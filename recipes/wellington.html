Beef Wellington

Description

Beef Wellington is a classic dish that features a tender beef fillet coated with a layer of pâté and wrapped in flaky puff pastry. It is often served with a rich red wine sauce and accompanied by seasonal vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 beef fillet (about 2 pounds)
  • 8 ounces of pâté (mushroom or liver)
  • 1 package of puff pastry (2 sheets)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for searing
  • Fresh thyme leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the beef fillet with salt and pepper, then sear it in a hot pan with olive oil until browned on all sides.
  3. Remove the beef from the pan and let it cool. Spread a layer of pâté over the cooled beef.
  4. Roll out the puff pastry sheets and wrap them around the beef, sealing the edges well.
  5. Brush the pastry with beaten egg for a golden finish.
  6. Place the wrapped beef on a baking sheet and bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving with red wine sauce and vegetables.
