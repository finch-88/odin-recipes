Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, meat mixture, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and marinara sauce. Repeat layers.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
