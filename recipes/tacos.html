Tacos

Description

Delicious and spicy tacos made with seasoned ground beef, fresh vegetables, and topped with cheese and salsa.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Salsa (to taste)
  • Olive oil (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spatula.
  3. Drain excess fat if necessary, then add the taco seasoning and water as per the packet instructions.
  4. Simmer for about 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. While the beef is cooking, prepare the taco shells according to package instructions.
  6. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with seasoned beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious tacos!
